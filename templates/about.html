About This Project

What is Crop Disease Identifier?

Crop Disease Identifier is an AI-powered web application designed to help farmers worldwide identify crop diseases instantly by uploading a photo of their crop. The system analyzes the image and provides disease information and treatment recommendations.

Our Mission

To empower farmers worldwide with modern AI technology to detect crop diseases early, reduce crop losses, and improve agricultural productivity.

How It Works

1. Farmer uploads a photo of a sick crop
2. Our AI analyzes the image instantly
3. System identifies the disease and severity
4. Treatment recommendations are provided
5. Results are saved for future reference

Technologies Used

Python & Flask (Backend), Groq AI - Llama 4 (AI Analysis), SQLite (Database), HTML, CSS & JavaScript (Frontend)
